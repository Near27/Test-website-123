Our Story

Born from a Love
of Coming Home

We started Maison Noir with a simple belief: the way your home smells changes the way it feels. That first breath when you walk through the door should feel like a warm embrace.

Every fragrance we create is hand-blended in small batches using ethically sourced ingredients. We obsess over every note, every layer, every transition — because we know that scent is the most intimate sense.

Our dark, elegant aesthetic reflects what we stand for: understated luxury that never shouts, but always lingers.
